We present the fabrication of a film-bulk-acoustic resonator (FBAR) with a highly c-axis- oriented AlN film on Mo/SiO2/Si (100) by using metal-organic chemical-vapor deposition (MOCVD).

The resonant frequency and the anti-resonant frequency of the fabricated resonator were 3.219 GHz and 3.249 GHz, respectively. The quality factor and the effective electromechanical coupling coefficient (k2eff) were 24.7 and 2.65%, respectively.
